How to Join Learnexus

Updated over 10 months ago

Learnexus is a members-only platform that connects the world's best L&D experts with high-paying work. Our clients include Fortune 1000 companies and leading non-profits.

Learnexus was built to help you work directly with exciting enterprise clients, earn top rates, get paid quickly, and build a steady pipeline of work. There are 3 steps to join:

Submit your profile

  • Create an account and fill out a profile with your skills, experience, and work you want to show off. Check out this guide to creating a stellar profile.

Complete a 15-minute video interview

  • Once you've submitted your profile, we'll email you a link to complete a 15-minute video interview where you can tell us more about your experience. Don't worry, the interview is easy and you can rerecord your answers before submitting.

  • Once you've submitted your profile and completed the interview, our team will conduct a one-time, required review for us to get to know you and better connect you to the right opportunities.

Get approved and find work

  • If our team thinks you're a good fit for the platform, they'll approve your profile. Once you're approved you can start easily finding work.
